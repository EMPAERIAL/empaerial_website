A strict black-and-white system built around two recurring shapes — the propeller and the wing-cut. Wide geometric type. Hairline rules over shadow. No emoji, no gradients, no decoration that doesn't earn its place.

Foundations

Tokens · type · motion

Palette

11-step ink scale, 3 paper tones, mirror inverse for dark surfaces. No color accents.

colors_and_type.css → --ink-* / --paper-*

Type

Saira (display, wide UPPERCASE), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (telemetry).

--font-display · --font-body · --font-mono

Spacing

4 px base. Pick small (4–16) or large (48–128). Skip the middle.

--sp-1 → --sp-32

Radii

Sharp 0 by default; pill 9999px for wing buttons + the Æ badge. Nothing in between.

--radius-sharp · --radius-pill

Elevation

Two shadows. Hairlines do most separation work.

--shadow-lift · --shadow-modal

Motion

Sharp, mechanical. 120 / 200 / 400 ms. cubic-bezier(0.2, 0, 0, 1) — propeller catching air.

--ease-prop · --dur-micro
